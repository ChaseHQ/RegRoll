RPG Registered Roll allows a GM to select a dice pool and hand off the dice to a player to roll. Once the roll has been completed it is permanently saved.

The GM has the right to pick difficulty dice to add to the pool or he may just wish to hand off a registered roll to the player. He also may add a description as a 'comment' to tell the player which ability dice pools he should roll with.

Once a Roll has been Generated the URL Link can be sent to the player and he may choose his appropriate dice pool and roll. Once a roll has been cast the link now just serves as a reference point and the player can no longer cast a roll for that link.
